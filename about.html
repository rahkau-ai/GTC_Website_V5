Background & Expertise

Dr. Rahul Kaushik is a distinguished gene therapy expert and neuroscientist with over 10 years of specialized experience in viral vector-based gene delivery targeting CNS, retina, and liver applications. As the Founder and CEO of Gene Therapy Consultancy, he provides strategic guidance to biotech companies navigating the complex path from promising AAV science to viable therapeutics.

Research & Development Experience

His extensive research background includes establishing rAAV and LVV platform technologies for gene supplementation, silencing, and editing, with production experience of over 150 rAAVs across different serotypes. Dr. Kaushik has been instrumental in three organizations dedicated to bringing transformative therapies to clinic, including a clinical-stage biotech in Munich with two AAV programs in clinical trials.

Scientific Contributions

With 16 peer-reviewed publications and over 1,000 citations, his research focuses on extracellular matrix regulation, synaptic plasticity, and neurodegeneration. His work has been published in prestigious journals including Science, Cell, and EMBO Journal. He holds certifications in gene technology, biosafety, and has multilingual capabilities in English, Hindi, and German.

Mission & Vision

Dr. Kaushik's mission through Gene Therapy Consultancy is to systematically remove "avoidable hurdles" in the gene therapy development process, helping clients navigate the "Valley of Death" between brilliant science and patient-reaching therapies. His proprietary knowledge platforms and expert networks provide data-driven strategic guidance for vector design, preclinical development, CMC strategy, and regulatory pathways.
