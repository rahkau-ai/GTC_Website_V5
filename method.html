The Foundation: Proprietary Intelligent Platforms

Our "Single Source of Truth" that powers reliable AI analysis

Not Just Databases—Knowledge Engines

Our Intelligent Platforms (VIP, PIP, CIP, CLIP, RIP, COIN) are structured knowledge engines that deconstruct every facet of gene therapy development. These continuously updated, human-curated systems provide the reliable foundation that enables our AI agents to deliver insights traditional consultancies simply cannot match.

VIP

Vector Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "Which vector will deliver the best therapeutic outcome for this specific target and indication?"

Comprehensive database of AAV vectors, serotypes, and performance characteristics across different tissues and applications.

  • Vector performance analytics
  • Tissue-specific tropism data
  • Manufacturing considerations
  • Regulatory precedents
  • Competitive landscape mapping
PIP

Preclinical Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "What is the most efficient and effective path to get this drug safely into human trials?"

Comprehensive repository of preclinical study designs, safety endpoints, dose-response relationships, and IND-enabling requirements across gene therapy programs.

  • IND-enabling study optimization
  • Safety assessment frameworks
  • Dose selection strategies
  • Regulatory readiness evaluation
  • Risk mitigation protocols
CIP

CMC Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "How can this drug be made reliably, scalably, and compliantly?"

Detailed database of manufacturing processes, analytical methods, quality control specifications, and scale-up strategies from research through commercial production.

  • Manufacturing process optimization
  • Scale-up strategy development
  • Quality control frameworks
  • Supply chain intelligence
  • Regulatory compliance assurance
CLIP

Clinical Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "What is the optimal clinical strategy to demonstrate safety and efficacy while minimizing risk and timeline?"

Structured analysis of clinical trials, outcomes, and regulatory interactions across the gene therapy landscape.

  • Trial design optimization
  • Endpoint selection guidance
  • Regulatory pathway analysis
  • Safety profile benchmarking
  • Market access insights
RIP

Regulatory Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "What do the regulators expect, and how can we proactively meet their standards to ensure the fastest, smoothest path to approval?"

Extensive collection of regulatory guidance documents, FDA/EMA meeting minutes, approval packages, and submission requirements specific to gene therapy products.

  • Regulatory pathway optimization
  • FDA/EMA interaction strategies
  • Submission requirement mapping
  • Approval precedent analysis
  • Regulatory risk assessment
COIN

Commercial Intelligence Platform

Core Question: "How does this drug become a successful business, not just a scientific achievement?"

Comprehensive market intelligence covering competitive landscapes, pricing strategies, reimbursement pathways, and go-to-market approaches for gene therapy products.

  • Market opportunity assessment
  • Competitive positioning analysis
  • Reimbursement strategy development
  • Go-to-market planning
  • Commercial viability modeling
